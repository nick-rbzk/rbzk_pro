About

When I was 18 I got my first pc. It was windows 98 and I exploded at first sight of it. I wish I never installed counter-strike on it because it completely messed with my then whispering passion to follow directory to directory in MS-DOS. I liked it.

I can still recall it to this day. Time passed, and even being older I still can’t see myself being happy without writing logic, anticipating what it does and having it do exactly what's expected. When one writes code it’s not the process of writing that excites. It’s finding a solution of a challenge and having a clear vision of how a particular solution is put together and implemented inside a machine. It’s the most amazing feeling I’ve experienced so far. And I can’t get enough of it!
